Amharic Bible Dictionary Privacy Policy

The Amharic Bible Dictionary is produced by Zelalem Mengistu. It was prepared over a 23-year period and completed in 2023. The dictionary covers over 4,800 terms defined with original Hebrew and Greek equivalents included for each entry.

Data Collection

The Amharic Bible Dictionary does not collect any personal data or information from its users. It does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

Data Sharing

The Amharic Bible Dictionary does not share any data with third parties. We prioritize your privacy and will never sell, trade, or disclose your personal information to external entities.

Usage Data

The Amharic Bible Dictionary may collect anonymous usage data, such as app analytics, to improve the user experience and app performance. However, this data does not include any personally identifiable information.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on this page.
